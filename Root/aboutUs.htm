Books and Tablets Staff

At Books and Tablets we recruit, develop and cultivate instructors with the highest qualifications, rich teaching experience and an aptitude for innovation. Please take a look at some of our talented teachers.

Mathematics Department

Jennifer Williams

Mathematics

Jennifer has been teaching math for over 10 years. Before working here at Books and Tablets, she worked as a teacher in Nebraska. Jennifer mainly teaches Algebra I, however has taken charge of geometry classes as well this year. Along with teaching, she leads a Math club and helps coach track here at our school.

Our mathematics teachers are well versed in all sections of mathematics from pre-algebra to advanced calculus.

  • Algebra
  • Advanced Algebra

  • College Algebra

Science Department

Scott Dunn

Biology

Scott Dunn has been with us for 15 years. He has taught many disciplines in science including chemistry and physics. He is a Saint Louis native, and went to school at UMSL. He now teaches biology here. Scott helps run Science Olympiad for our school. Our Science Olympiad has had much success since Scott started leading the team.

The science courses at Books and Tablets lead students through a very thorough exploration of the core sciences, and culminate in what we feel are an excellent choice of electives in junior and senior year.

Science Offerings

  • Biology
  • Chemistry
  • Physics
  • Environmental Science
  • Anatomy and Physiology

Social Studies Department

Lisa Brown

American History

Lisa has served as our history teacher for 5 years now. She graduated from the University of Kansas and moved to Saint Louis with her family to start working here. Lisa has a great passion for history and while she focuses on American History, she has also taught World History, and European History for us. As an avid sports fan Lisa helps coach our girls' basketball team as well as our girls' swim team.

Our Social Studies Department helps students develop a better understanding and appreciation of history, geography, economic and political systems, and civic participation. In order to prepare students to succeed in a rapidly changing global society, Books and Tablets focuses on the development of skills such as problem solving, collaboration, and critical thinking.

Social Studies Offerings

  • American History
  • World History
  • U.S. Government and Politics
  • Personal Finance
  • Economics
  • U.S. Law
