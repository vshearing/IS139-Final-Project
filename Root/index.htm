The Books and Tablets Mission Statement

Books and Tablets provides high quality education and choice of learning: virtually or in-person for students in grades 8 through 12.

Our Philosophy

At Books and Tablets we believe that all children are unique and must have a stimulating educational environment where they can grow physically, mentally, emotionally, and socially. It is our desire to create this type of atmosphere where students can meet their full potential. We will provide a safe environment where students are invited to share their ideas and take risks.

We believe that there are five essential elements that are conducive to learning. (1) The teacher's role is to act as a guide. (2) Students must have access to hands-on activities. (3) Students should be able to have choices and let their curiosity direct their learning. (4) Students need the opportunity to practice skills in a safe environment. (5) Technology must be incorporated into the school day."

Why Choose Books and Tablets?

The Books and Tablets is a flexible learning environment that blends in-person and virtual learning. We strive to give our students the best education possible that fits their learning styles. Here are a few things that set us apart:

  • Small Class Size Most of our classes have 10-12 students.
  • Highly Qualified Instructors Our staff includes young, energetic innovative teachers as well as some of the most experienced and respected instructors in the area.
  • Peer Support Our peer mentoring and support system helps new students become part of our community and helps the older students develop leadership skills.
  • Community Outreach We have extensive ties with the community through service projects, character education, community events, etc.
  • Open Campus Policy Our students can leave campus for lunch, they also have study hour embedded into the school day for academic support, labs, tutoring and other tasks.
